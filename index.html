About Us

Why come to us?

TPI Taping and Jointing are a very reliable, fast and efficient taping and jointing contractor you can trust. TPi are always one step ahead to make sure work is carried out safely, correctly and legally.

TPi are fully insured as a company and are CHAS, FIS and Construction line accredited. Our on site supervisors are SSSTS and First Aid at work trained and our operatives have CSCS skilled worker cards, IPAF, PASMA licenses and drywall sanding machines with M class dust extractors for a fast and safe sand.
